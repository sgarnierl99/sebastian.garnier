School Projects

  • Company Fundamental Analysis

    I carried out a fundamental analysis of Visa Inc. using both top-down and bottom-up approaches. Starting with an industry review and competitor comparison, I assessed Visa’s position in the payments sector. I then applied valuation models such as CAPM, intrinsic value, EPS, and P/E ratio. Finally, I compared my valuation with the market price to determine if the stock appeared over- or undervalued, connecting theory with real market behavior.

  • Portfolio Analysis Assignment

    As part of a portfolio management project, I analyzed Ford (F), Tesla (TSLA), and other securities to apply corporate finance and investment theory. I collected and processed financial data, calculated risk and return metrics, and used models such as CAPM and portfolio optimization to evaluate performance. This project strengthened my ability to apply theoretical frameworks to real data, building both analytical and quantitative skills in investment analysis and portfolio construction.
